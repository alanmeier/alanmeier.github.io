To Kill A Dead Man is the sonic project of Sydney producers Alex Middleton and Jessica Violet. After meeting on the bus as teenagers and bonding over their mutual love for sound, the two have been swapping song recommendations and field recordings ever since.

Their mutual obsession with sound takes many forms, To Kill A Dead Man not only encompasses their own releases but also their original compositions for film, field recordings and work together as sound designers.

Using sound to tell stories, Middleton and Violet have soundtracked creative content for clients including Under Amour, Culture Machine and Schwarzkopf.
